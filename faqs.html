Frequently Asked Questions

Booking information on Motorbike rental For booking bike tours in Nepal or Renting Motorbike on your requested date. We need a 40% advance payment from the total payment to reserve the Motorbike. Reservation payment is nonrefundable. Payment can be done with various methods, such as available payment systems in Nepal like Western Union, Money gram, IME or Bank transfer, etc.
  • Full payment should be done before renting the Motorbike.
  • Payment can be done either in Nepalese rupees or in the dollar.
  • We don't accept credit cards.

2. What should I submit for Rental?

  • Valid Driving license from your country or International Driving license for renting Motorbike.
  • Deposit of your Original Passport with Nepalese Visa. Or You need to have someone to Stay as a guarantor from Nepal in case if you don't want to deposit your passport.
  • or refundable Cash Deposit according to Bike. (Depends on the bike from USD 500 up to USD 1000 refundable cash required to rent a motorbike.)
  • and Submission of Citizenship Card photocopy of Identity Card or any other card which has your personal details is required.

3. What liscence is required for Motorbike Rental ?

  • Rider must have a Motorcycle driving license to rent a bike or touring with us. It's not allowed riding a motorbike with a Car Driving license in Nepal.

4. What is included on the Motorcycle Rental price ?

  • Only helmet and bike are included in the price. (Fuel is not included in rental price).
  • We provide only 2/3 ltr of fuel before handling bike for Rentals. Which is enough to go any nearby fuel pump.

5. What happen if i paid for advance payment for reservation of Bike rental and cancel the trip?

  • We required a 40% advance to make a reservation for bike rental, once you cancel your trip, the 40% payment won't be refunded.

6. What happen if i paid full amount for my Rentals and return bike early?

  • Suppose you rent a bike for 10 days, we required a total payment of 10 days and you return the bike within 5 days, Payment won't be refunded since we have to cancel other bookings
  • If you are sure to rent for 5 days and you wish to add 5 more days. Just write to us an email or give us a phone call and you can pay when returning the bike.

7. Regarding Insurance Policy?

  • Insurance is not provided for Rental bikes in Nepal. If in due course of a rental time bike accident, loss, scratches, broken parts, physical damage, etc. occurred the insurance is not applicable.
  • Renter should bear the cost, in case if bike lost, broken, scratch, physical damage, etc during the rental period.
  • We request renter take a video/photo of the bike before collecting the bike.

8. What Gears are required for Motorbike tours?

  • Riding Gears:

    1. Sunglasses, Sunblock, lip cream, moisturizers, passport copies, Neck-warmer while driving to high altitude
    2. Riding trousers & jacket (with winter liner or sweater underneath)
    3. Proper Riding boots/hiking boots
    4. Riding Gloves
    5. Helmets
    6. Mask, Muffler/goggles
    7. Thick warm Shocks to avoid cold
    8. Other Personal items as desire.

  • For overnight:

    1. Sandals/comfortable shoes
    2. Fleece/sweater & warm jacket
    3. Comfy pants/trackies
    4. A pair of thermals may be comfortable if you feel the cold
    5. Light towel
    6. T-shirts
    7. Personal toiletries
    8. Electronic items (GoPro, camera, phone, etc)
